This folder mirrors the restorable parts of C:\Users\user\.codex.

Included:
- top-level config-style files such as *.toml, *.yaml, *.yml, *.md, *.rules
- full directories: agents, skills, plugins, rules, memories, automations

Excluded on purpose:
- auth/session/secrets files
- app-global state and migration markers
- sqlite databases, WAL/SHM files, logs
- caches, sandbox temp data, generated images, archived sessions

To refresh this backup, run:
pwsh -File .\scripts\sync-codex-home.ps1
